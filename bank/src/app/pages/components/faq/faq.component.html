FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • What is the purpose of this Portal?

    This portal provides an enhanced user experience through seamless single window access to information by search based on the type and location of property put up for e-auction by Indian banks. The common platform also provides for smooth navigation to the bank e-auction site once a notified property is selected.

  • Does a user require registration/login to use the portal?

    User can directly access the portal using single sign on option from google, facebook or they can register as new customer using username and password.

  • What benefits are derived from this portal?

    Potential bidders can select segmented data bank-wise and as per location (States and Districts) across banks.

  • Is nature of ownership of property given?

    Information of the nature of ownership in the categories freehold, lease hold, cooperative society and others is available on the portal.

  • Is property classified in terms of its nature of use?

    Yes, property is searchable across residential & commercial Properties

  • What type of Commercial property information is available?

    Commercial property information is available with regard to type of property: Office, Shop, Mall, Godown, Plot

  • What comprises property details as provided by the banks?

    Property type, location, number of rooms ( in residential property) and type of ownership and reserve price range will enable bidders to compare similar properties. properties of interest from the displayed list by clicking to view the bank e auction date, reserve price and property address and description. Photographs of properties that have been uploaded can also be viewed.

  • Can bank property be bought without Auction ?

    Yes, it is definitely possible. Our team would assist in closing the deal on your behalf.
